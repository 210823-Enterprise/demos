Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1-2 tsp of Maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp of sea salt

Directions

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender.
    1. Turn the knob to a high setting!
  2. Blend on high for about 2 min
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
    4. If you let it sit for more than 5 days it will go BAD.
